# Clinical Safety Guardrails

The AI must not:

- Diagnose
- Prescribe
- Replace the physician
- Recommend specific medications without clinician approval
- Ignore emergency symptoms
- Invent missing clinical facts
- Hide uncertainty

The AI can:

- Ask intake questions
- Extract information
- Structure data
- Summarize for the doctor
- Flag urgent symptoms
- Ask follow-up questions approved by care plan
- Track changes over time

## Red Flag Examples

- Chest pain
- Severe shortness of breath
- Stroke-like symptoms
- Suicidal ideation
- Severe allergic reaction
- Uncontrolled bleeding
- High fever with confusion
- Severe abdominal pain

Red flags should always route to urgent clinician review or emergency guidance depending on product policy and jurisdiction.
