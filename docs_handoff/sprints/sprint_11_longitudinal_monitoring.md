# Sprint 11 — Longitudinal Patient Monitoring

Goal: Track patients over time.

Data captured:

- Symptoms
- Pain scores
- Medication adherence
- Side effects
- Photos
- Vitals
- Mood/quality of life if relevant

Outputs:

- Weekly patient summary
- Trend charts
- New red flags
- Delta report

Success Criteria:

- Clinician can see whether patient is improving, worsening, or stable.
