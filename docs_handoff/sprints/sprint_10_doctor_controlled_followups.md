# Sprint 10 — Doctor-Controlled Follow-up Plans

Goal: Follow-up workflows are triggered by doctor decisions.

Examples:

Self-care:
- Pain score
- Fever
- Symptom improvement
- New red flags

Medication monitoring:
- Adherence
- Side effects
- Improvement

Post-procedure:
- Pain
- Fever
- Wound photo
- Bleeding

Success Criteria:

- Follow-ups are personalized to the care plan, not generic reminders.
