# Sprint 8 — Clinical Flagging Engine

Goal: Prioritize patients using red/yellow/green logic.

Red:

- Emergency symptoms
- Severe worsening
- Dangerous medication/allergy issues

Yellow:

- Persistent symptoms
- Medication non-adherence
- Mild worsening

Green:

- Stable or improving

Success Criteria:

- High-risk patients rise to the top of the doctor queue.
